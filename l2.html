Lesson 2 ‚Äì Types and Sources of Customer Data

Introduction

In Customer Analytics, data is the most important element. Without data, there is nothing to analyze. Understanding the types of customer data and where it comes from helps businesses collect useful information that leads to better decisions.

Types of Customer Data

  • Demographic Data: Basic personal information about the customer (e.g., age, gender, income, education, location).
  • Behavioral Data: How customers act when engaging with a brand (e.g., browsing history, purchase frequency, time spent on website).
  • Transactional Data: Information about purchases or financial activities (e.g., items bought, total spending, payment method, purchase date).
  • Psychographic Data: Data about lifestyle, interests, values, and opinions (e.g., hobbies, personality traits, product preferences).

Summary:

  • Demographic ‚Üí Who the customer is
  • Behavioral ‚Üí What the customer does
  • Transactional ‚Üí What the customer buys
  • Psychographic ‚Üí Why the customer chooses it
