Touch Typing Reinforcement

  • Maintain correct home-row hand position.
  • Avoid looking at the keyboard; focus on the screen.
  • Use assigned fingers for each key for maximum speed and accuracy.

Error Correction

  • Use Backspace or Delete for quick corrections.
  • Identify recurring errors and adjust technique accordingly.
  • Develop self-monitoring while typing long texts.

2. Ergonomics

Ergonomics improves comfort, prevents injury, and enhances typing performance.

Proper Chair and Desk Setup

  • Feet flat on the floor, knees at a 90¬∞ angle.
  • Desk height allows forearms to be parallel to the floor.
  • Monitor at eye level, 20‚Äì28 inches away.

Posture and Finger Placement

  • Sit upright with relaxed shoulders.
  • Wrists slightly elevated, not resting on the table.
  • Fingers curved over home row keys.

Breaks and Exercises

  • Take breaks every 30‚Äì45 minutes.
  • Stretch fingers, wrists, neck, and shoulders.
  • Do hand flexibility exercises to prevent RSI.

3. Efficient Long-Document Strategies

  • Use keyboard shortcuts to reduce time and effort.
  • Break long documents into manageable sections.
  • Proofread each section before moving on.
  • Maintain consistent formatting to avoid extra editing.
