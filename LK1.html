What is Keyboarding?

Keyboarding is the skill of entering text through a keyboard accurately, efficiently, and comfortably.

  • It is fundamental in Office Administration because typing is required for letters, reports, emails, and data entry.
  • Good keyboarding involves minimal errors, proper speed, and reduced fatigue.

Brief History / Evolution

  • The typewriter came before the computer keyboard and established the QWERTY layout.
  • Modern keyboards evolved from mechanical to membrane to ergonomic designs.
  • Despite advancements, the QWERTY layout remains the most widely used.

Importance in Office Administration

  • Employers expect administrative staff to type with reasonable speed and accuracy.
  • Efficient typing saves time and reduces errors.
  • It supports major tasks like document creation, email communication, and data encoding.
